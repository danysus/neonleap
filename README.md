Escape the Void in Cube Crush! Take control of a high-speed, neon-charged cube in this minimalist endless runner. The floor is shifting, the spikes are sharp, and the speed never stops. How far can you travel into the darkness before the crush catches up?

Features:

Infinite Procedural Levels: No two runs are ever the same.
The Shop: Collect gold coins to unlock 5 unique skins, including the legendary Gold Skin.
Wall Jumping: Master the art of the wall kick to survive tricky gaps.
Save System: Your coins and high scores are saved automatically, even in the browser!
Retro Aesthetics: Clean, high-contrast visuals designed for focus and flow.
Controls:

Move: A / D or Left / Right Arrows
Jump / Wall Jump: Space, W, or Up Arrow
Double Jump: Hit jump again while in mid-air!
